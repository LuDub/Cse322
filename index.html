What is a bomb?

A bomb is an older vehicle typically from the 1930s to the Early 50s. Bombs usually have modified suspension, numerous auto accessories, and lots of chrome parts. The reason for these cars being called Bombs is due to the era they are from. Mainly in the 1940s to Early 50s it is known as the bomb era due to many nuclear bomb scares. Also many of these vehicles have a vintage bomb-like shape to them. Still on the road to this day with their beautiful vintage style, Bringing the past into the future. Bombs keep history alive.
